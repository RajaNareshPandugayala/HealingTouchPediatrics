Immunizations


We require our patients to be vaccinated as recommended by the Advisory Committee on Immunization Practices (ACIP), the American Academy of Pediatrics (AAP), the American Academy of Family Physicians (ACFP) and the American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists (ACOG). We can accommodate an alternate schedule upon request.
