Message from Dr. Sirikonda

Dr. Purnachander Sirikonda, is the Owner and President of Healing Touch Pediatrics (also DBA as Collins Street Pediatrics). He is an alumni of Gandhi Medical College, Osmania University, Hyderabad and also did 2 years of Residency in Pediatrics before migrating with immigration to USA.

After completion of his Residency in Morristown Memorial Hospital, Morristown, NJ affiliated with Columbia University, he had worked with Hackensack University Medical Center for 5 years as Peds ER doctor and Hospitalist and in 2001-2018 Dr Sirikonda has his own 4 practices in NJ.

In 2018 Dr Sirikonda bought and took over Collins Street Pediatrics. And this office in business for 8-9 years. Collins Street Pediatrics was founded on the desire to be the best pediatric practice in the area.
