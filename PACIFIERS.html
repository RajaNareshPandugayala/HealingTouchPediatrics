The Use of PACIFIERS

The use of a pacifier is a parental choice. They can be a soothing tool for your baby. Nutritive sucking is what the baby uses to feed and grow. Non-nutritive sucking is very soothing activity for babies. Pacifiers can help with this and can help if you have a fussy baby.

Early pacifier use may decrease the risk of SIDS.
