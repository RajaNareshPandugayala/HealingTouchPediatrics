Pre - Natal Care

Are you expecting? we provide complimentary pre-natal consultations

Pre - Natal Consult

A prenatal visit with Dr. Sirikonda is the perfect opportunity to start a relationship with us, you, and your little one. This visit is a great chance to answer many common questions first-time and expectant parents have. During your free prenatal visit with Collins Street Pediatrics we will typically cover the following topics:

  • Office Hours
  • How and when to contact the Nurses and Physicians
  • Availability of same day sick visits
  • Office protocols regarding vaccines and well visit care
  • Availability of the on-call doctor for after hours phone calls.

Additional Benifits of the Prenatal visit:
  • Learn about the doctor's phylosophy on pediatric care.
  • Tour the office and meet our staff.
  • Ask questions.
  • Become comfortable with getting to the office and the registration process.
